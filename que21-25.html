It provides not only the essential resources for survival—like clean air, fresh water, and nourishing food—but also serves as a source of inspiration, balance, and healing. The vibrant colors of blooming flowers, the soothing sounds of birdsong, and the calm rustle of leaves in the wind create an atmosphere that refreshes the mind and soul.
